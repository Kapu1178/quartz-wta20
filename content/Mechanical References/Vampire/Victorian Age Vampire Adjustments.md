## Generation
The purity of the Kindred blood has not yet begun to falter. In this last great age of vampiric power, when Kindred can still maintain private circles of undead society in their palatial estates, powerful sires are more common. The fears of weaker generations are merest of rumor. The vampires of these nights are potent and confident. The worst times are still a generation off, after all.
